Aphrodites Rock

This is one of the most famous spots in Pafos, a coastal city in Cyprus. It is also known as Petra tou Romiou, which means

'Rock of the Greek.' Its popularity is linked with Greek mythology and the belief that Aphrodite—the goddess of love,

beauty, procreation and pleasure—was born here. There is a belief that swimming around Aphrodite's rock brings one

eternal beauty.
